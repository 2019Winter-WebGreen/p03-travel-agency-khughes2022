Touring Maldives



Maldives is a beautiful country located in South America. There are over 1200 islands incorporated in the country. There are so many actvities to do and so many places to go. Maldives is a great place to visit with your family, friends, spouse, or even alone for a relaxing vacation!


Location

The Capital of Maldives is Male. Maldives stretches 510 miles from North to South and 80 miles East to North. The Islands can be found in the Indian Ocean. Barrier reefs surround the Islands to protect them from monsoons. The temperature is on the lower side for a beachy Island. The temperature ranges from 76 degrees to 86 degrees Farenheit. (24 degrees - 30 degrees Celcius) The population of Maldives as of this year, 2019, is about 451,783 people.


Attractions

There are so many fun things to do in Maldives. Geography, sports, relaxation trips, food, Religion; you name, they have it.
